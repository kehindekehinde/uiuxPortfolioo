About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Kenny I have a passion for building modern, data-driven solutions that provide accessible delight to the human experience and specialize in responsive design across mobile, tablet and web. “There has to be a better way” are words I have lived by for my entire life. These words have fueled my successes in my past roles resulting in implementation of novel organizational protocols and cross functional team alignment. I credit this success to my need to find and test creative solutions to save time, energy, and money. My career has always been fueled by a desire to understand and connect with people from various backgrounds and my experiences in those roles has developed my eagerness to harness human psychology to build processes that feel tailored. To me, a product’s quality is rooted in its simplicity and efficiency. I believe being a student is a mindset that never ends. I thrive being thrown into a sink or swim environment as I am a self starter through and through and always find the answers I don’t know. After dealing with humans directly for so many years I've realized UX design is my calling. I live to provide function and beauty to the every day for all.
